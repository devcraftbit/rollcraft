Balancing the Rules

A game must be fair. We playtest hundreds of times. If a card is too strong, we change it. If a rule is confusing, we rewrite it. Simple is always better.

  • We cut rules that slow things down.
  • We make turns fast and snappy.
  • We ensure everyone stays engaged.
